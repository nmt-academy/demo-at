Bridge Messenger
CPO & Co-founder
Jul 2024 – Sep 2025
The insight
40M+ workers in Eastern Europe operate in low-bandwidth environments where mainstream messengers fail. We built for them — secure, lightweight, offline-first.

As CPO, I owned the full product: UX, feature prioritisation, go-to-market. We became the first team ever to complete the UCU Startup Accelerator while still in high school — running the programme alongside final exams and 27 hours of weekly tutoring sessions I ran myself.

What I learned
Distribution beats features. We grew to 7k users through community trust, not ads. And hardware constraints force better product decisions than unlimited resources ever would.

NMT.Academy
CEO & Co-founder
Jul 2024 – Present
The insight
350,000+ Ukrainian students sit the NMT exam each year. Existing prep is generic and offline. We built a data-driven, simulation-first platform that adapts to each student's weak spots.

Started as a side project while running Bridge — one teacher, one subject. Grew organically through results: our school reached #2 in Ukraine for History NMT scores. Now expanding faculty to make the platform nationwide.

What I learned
Measurable outcomes are the only marketing you need in education. Every student who scored well became a referral. Build the result, the growth follows.
